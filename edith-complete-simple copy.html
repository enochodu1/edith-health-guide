üò£ YOUR SYMPTOMS - What's Causing Them

SEVERE BLOATING (stomach very full, hard to move)

Caused by:

  • Ozempic shot (slows stomach)
  • No thyroid (slows digestion)
  • Tylenol #3 pain pill (stops gut movement)
  • Eating fried food (sits in stomach 12 hours)

What helps:

  • Eat tiny meals (5-6 small meals, not 3 big)
  • NO fried food
  • Walk 10 minutes after eating
  • Stop eating 3 hours before bed

CAN'T WALK / WEAK LEGS

Caused by:

  • Low potassium (water pill flushes it out)
  • Too much weight on knees
  • Thyroid pills not working (if you eat cassava)
  • Legs swollen (too much salt)

What helps:

  • Get potassium blood test (URGENT)
  • Eat avocado, oranges, kontomire (high potassium)
  • NO salty food
  • Lose weight (will help knees)

SEVERE CONSTIPATION (can't poop)

Caused by:

  • Tylenol #3 (codeine) - MAIN CAUSE
  • Ozempic
  • No thyroid (slows everything)

What helps:

  • Take Miralax EVERY DAY (not just when constipated)
  • Take Docusate (stool softener) 2x daily
  • Eat okra every day
  • Drink 8 glasses of water
  • Walk after meals
