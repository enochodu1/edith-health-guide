ü•Ñ BEST FOODS FOR CONSTIPATION RELIEF

1. PRUNES (THE #1 SOLUTION!)

How much: 5-7 prunes daily (morning or night)
Why it works: Contains sorbitol (natural laxative) + high fiber. Works better than prescription laxatives!
Results: Bowel movement within 12-24 hours
How to eat: Plain, or make prune juice smoothie

2. WARM WATER (First thing in morning)

How much: 1-2 glasses (8-16 oz) immediately when you wake up
Why it works: Stimulates bowel movement, softens stool
Add: Squeeze of lemon for extra help

3. PAPAYA (African Pawpaw)

How much: 1 cup ripe papaya daily
Why it works: Contains papain enzyme that aids digestion
Best time: With breakfast or as snack

4. OKRA SOUP

How much: 1 bowl daily
Why it works: Mucilage (slime) lubricates intestines
Tip: Cook well, eat the slippery part!

5. BEANS (Black-eyed peas, Red beans)

How much: 1/2 cup cooked beans 4x per week
Why it works: Very high fiber (15g per cup)
Tip: Cook soft, mash slightly if needed

6. SPINACH & KONTOMIRE (Cooked)

How much: 1-2 cups daily (remember: eat 1 hour AFTER thyroid pills!)
Why it works: High fiber + magnesium relaxes intestines
Best way: Steamed or in soup

7. ORANGES & GRAPEFRUIT

How much: 1 orange daily
Why it works: Pectin fiber + vitamin C + water
Eat: The whole fruit (not juice) to get fiber

8. FLAXSEED (Ground)

How much: 1 tablespoon in water or soup daily
Why it works: Swells in intestines, adds bulk
IMPORTANT: Drink 8 oz water immediately after!
