1. Low-Salt Kontomire Soup

Ingredients:
‚Ä¢ 2 bunches kontomire (cocoyam leaves), washed and chopped
‚Ä¢ 2 fresh tomatoes, blended
‚Ä¢ 1 onion, diced
‚Ä¢ 2 pieces fish (tilapia or mackerel)
‚Ä¢ 1 tbsp peanut oil
‚Ä¢ Fresh pepper to taste
‚Ä¢ Herbs: basil, thyme, garlic (instead of Maggi cubes!)
Steps:
1. Heat oil in pot, add onions and garlic
2. Add blended tomatoes and fresh pepper, cook 5 min
3. Add water (2 cups) and herbs
4. Add kontomire leaves, cook 10 minutes
5. Add fish, cook another 5 minutes
6. Serve with small yam fufu

Ingredients:
‚Ä¢ 2 unripe plantains, peeled and cut
‚Ä¢ 1 cup spinach, chopped
‚Ä¢ 200g fish (mackerel or sardines)
‚Ä¢ 1 tbsp olive oil
‚Ä¢ 1 onion, diced

Steps:

3. Add spinach, onions, and pepper

5. Cook together 5 more minutes
6. Serve hot

‚Ä¢ 2 cups fresh okra, sliced (or dried okra soaked)
